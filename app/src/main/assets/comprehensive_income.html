Comprehensive Income


In this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Identify the elements of the SCI and describe each of these items.
  • Prepare an SCI for a service business.
  • Prepare an SCI for a merchandising business.

Key Elements of the Statement of Comprehensive Income (SCI):

  1. Net Income: This represents the profit or loss generated by a company from its primary business activities. It reflects the company's financial performance.
  2. Other Comprehensive Income (OCI): OCI includes gains or losses that are not part of the company's core operations. Examples include foreign currency translation adjustments, unrealized gains or losses on securities, and pension plan adjustments.

Preparing an SCI for a Service Business in the Philippines:

  1. Identify Revenue: Calculate the total revenue generated from providing services.
  2. Deduct Operating Expenses: Subtract operating expenses like salaries, rent, utilities, and other costs directly related to providing services.
  3. Calculate Net Income: Determine the net income by subtracting total expenses from total revenue.
  4. Compute Other Comprehensive Income: If applicable, include items like foreign currency adjustments or other non-operational gains or losses.
