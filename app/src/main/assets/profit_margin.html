Keypoints:

Formula: Net Profit Margin = (Net Income / Revenue) * 100%

Units: Ensure consistency – express revenue and net income in the same units (e.g., dollars).

Comparison: Benchmark your margin against industry averages or competitors to assess your financial health.

Estimating: Use rough calculations like "Profit Margin ≈ Price – Cost of Goods Sold / Price * 100%" for quick insights.

Shortcuts: Employ online calculators, industry reports, and financial software for efficient analysis.

Sample Question:

A bakery sells cupcakes for $3 each and incurs a production cost of $1.50 per cupcake. Their monthly revenue is $18,000. What is their monthly net profit margin?

Solution:

Net Income = Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold = $18,000 - ($1.50/cupcake * 6,000 cupcakes) = $6,000

Net Profit Margin = (Net Income / Revenue) * 100% = ($6,000 / $18,000) * 100% = 33.33%
